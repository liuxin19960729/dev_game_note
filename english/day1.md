## add
```
one  add  one (1+1)

add up sth 把什么东西全部加起来
    例 add up to  all the numbers ;//把所有的数加起来
sth add up to sth 什么加起来是什么
  例 all the numbers add up to 100; //所有的数加起来的和是100




addition (n) 加法,附加物
    in addition 另外
    连接词用(介词)
       We went shopping  and  in  addition  saw  a movie ;
       我们去购物 另外我们还看了电影  
    in addition to 除....之外
     in addition to + 除了干什么事，+还敢什么事

    In addition to  going shopping, we saw a movie 


additional (adj) 附加的

  In addition to the words above ,I will teach  you  some 
    In addition to + 除之外的事     除...之外 
  additional new words ;
  additional adj 附加的


```

## all (所有 一切 全部)

```
all of you  所有人  大家（和 everyone 意思相同)


All men (所有人) want to live along  but all men must die;


above all (在所有的上面) 首先

after all (在所有的后面 最后进行总结)  毕竟 总结
例 After all I am still a child ;毕竟我还是一个孩子


all in all (全部里面的全部)  总的来说
 例 All in all ,my condition is greatly improved;
      总体上来说我的情况好多了


all over 到处 遍地

Treveling(adj 旅行) all over the word,is my dream;



at all 丝毫  用于否定句

```
